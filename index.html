Walks of Paws

Welcome to Walks of Paws, where we are passionate about providing the highest level of care and attention to your furry family members. Our experienced team prioritizes your dog's well-being and offers a range of services tailored to their needs, including group walks, solo walks, and puppy visits. Whether your dog is young and energetic or older and more laid-back, we have the expertise to ensure that they have a great time with us. Contact us today to learn more and trust us to provide the love and attention your furry friend deserves.

SERVICES

Group Walks

Our experienced dog walkers carefully group dogs based on their temperament, energy level, and size, providing a safe and stimulating environment for dogs to socialize, learn important social skills, and get exercise while in the care of our attentive and experienced team.

Solo Walks

Our attentive team of experienced dog walkers tailor solo walks to your dog's individual needs and preferences, providing them with the one-on-one attention they deserve for a fun, safe, and personalized walking experience. They ensure that each dog receives their personalized attention, tailored to their needs.

Puppy visits

Our personalized and flexible puppy visit schedule is designed to fit your puppy's needs and your schedule, and our experienced dog walkers provide customized visits that give your new furry family member plenty of love, attention, and assistance with potty training and socialization.

ABOUT US

Our team consists of dedicated dog lovers who provide personalized care and attention to your furry friends. Our professional dog walkers are fully trained and insured, and have a wealth of experience in handling all types of dogs. We understand that each dog is unique, which is why we offer a range of services tailored to their individual needs and preferences. From group walks to solo walks and puppy visits, our team of passionate dog walkers will ensure your dog receives the attention, exercise and stimulation they need to stay happy and healthy. With Walks of Paws, you can rest assured that your furry family member is in loving hands. Join us for an enjoyable, safe and personalized walking experience for your dog.
